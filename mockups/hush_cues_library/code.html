Hands Trust

Open Palms

Keeping your palms visible and open signals honesty and non-threat. It's a primal cue that builds subconscious trust immediately in negotiations or casual introductions.

tips_and_updates Linda's Tip

Avoid pointing. Instead, gesture towards objects or people with an open hand, palm slightly up.

The 50/70 Rule

Face

Maintain eye contact 50% of the time while speaking and 70% while listening. This demonstrates active engagement without becoming intensely confrontational.

Subtle Mirroring

Posture

Gently matching the posture or energy level of your conversation partner builds rapport. It signals empathy and creates a feeling of 'being on the same page'.

The 'V' Stance

Posture Authority

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, weight evenly distributed. This grounded stance projects stability and confidence, preventing nervous swaying.
